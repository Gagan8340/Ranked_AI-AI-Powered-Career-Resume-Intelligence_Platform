Professional Resume Templates

Select a LaTeX template to launch the editor and build your resume.

Before you spend hours customizing a template, run it past this checklist.
If it has more than one or two items from the right column, pick a different template.

GREEN FLAGS — ATS-Safe

  • Single-column layout
  • Standard section headings (Experience, Education, Skills)
  • Black text on a white background
  • One clean font throughout (Arial, Calibri, Helvetica)
  • Plain round or hyphen bullet points
  • Dates clearly aligned to the right of each role
  • Contact info in the body, not the header/footer
  • Saved as .docx or text-based .pdf
  • All text selectable when opened (not images)

RED FLAGS — Likely Rejected

  • Two- or three-column designs
  • Sidebars with skills, contact info, or a photo
  • Tables (even invisible ones used for layout)
  • Text inside text boxes or decorative shapes
  • Name and contact details in the header/footer
  • Color blocks or background images behind text
  • Icons next to phone, email, or location
  • Progress bars or star ratings for skills
  • Profile photo, decorative fonts, mixed font families
